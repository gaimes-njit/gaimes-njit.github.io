To create an "L" shape structure inside a 20*16 box, you can use the following sequence of ab_drop commands:

1. ab_drop('b31', 0) - This will drop a 3*1 block at position (0, 0).
2. ab_drop('b11', 0) - This will drop a 1*1 block at position (0, 1).
3. ab_drop('b11', 0) - This will drop a 1*1 block at position (0, 2).
4. ab_drop('b13', 0) - This will drop a 1*3 block at position (0, 3).
