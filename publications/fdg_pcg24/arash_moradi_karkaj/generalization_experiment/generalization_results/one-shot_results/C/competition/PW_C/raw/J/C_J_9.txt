To generate the uppercase English letter "J", we can use the following sequence of ab_drop() commands:

ab_drop('b31', 1)  // Place the foundation of the letter (horizontal straight block) at position 1
ab_drop('b13', 1)  // Place the vertical straight block on the left side of the foundation at position 1
ab_drop('b13', 2)  // Place another vertical straight block on the left side of the foundation at position 2
ab_drop('b31', 2)  // Place the top part of the letter (horizontal straight block) at position 2
