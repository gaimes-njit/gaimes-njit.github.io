To create the L-shaped structure inside a 20*16 box, you can use the following ab_drop commands:

1. Place a single block (b11) at position (0, 0):
ab_drop('b11', 0)

2. Place a horizontal block (b31) at position (0, 1):
ab_drop('b31', 0)

3. Place a vertical block (b13) at position (2, 0):
ab_drop('b13', 2)

You should now have an L-shaped structure inside the 20*16 box.
