To create the uppercase letter "A" in the Tetris-like environment, we can use the following ab_drop() commands:

```
ab_drop('b31',1)
ab_drop('b13',0)
ab_drop('b13',2)
ab_drop('b31',1)
ab_drop('b11',0)
ab_drop('b11',1)
ab_drop('b11',2)
```

Explanation:

- The first ab_drop('b31',1) command creates the top horizontal line of the "A".
- Then, ab_drop('b13',0) and ab_drop('b13',2) commands create the two vertical lines of the "A".
- The next ab_drop('b31',1) command creates the bottom horizontal line of the "A".
- Finally, three ab_drop('b11',0), ab_drop('b11',1), and ab_drop('b11',2) commands create the middle line of the "A".

Please note that the ab_drop() commands should be executed consecutively to properly construct the letter "A" and ensure its stability within the defined environment.
