To generate the uppercase English letter "R", the following stream of ab_drop() commands can be used:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b11', 7)
ab_drop('b11', 8)
ab_drop('b11', 9)
ab_drop('b11', 10)
```

This will create an uppercase "R" shape using the block types b11, b31, and b13 in the defined environment.
