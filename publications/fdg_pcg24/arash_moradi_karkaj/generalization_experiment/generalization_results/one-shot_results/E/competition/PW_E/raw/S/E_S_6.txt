To generate the uppercase English letter "S", you can use the following ab_drop() commands:

``` 
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
```

Please note that the commands should be executed consecutively, starting from the top-left corner of the grid (position 1).
