To create the structure exactly like "L" inside a 20*16 box, you can use the following ab_drop commands:

1. Place the top horizontal block of the "L" (b31) starting at position (0,0):
   ab_drop('b31', 0)

2. Place the vertical part of the "L" (b13) starting at position (0,2):
   ab_drop('b13', 2)
