To generate the uppercase English letter "O", we can use the following ab_drop() commands:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
```

This sequence of commands will drop the block types in a way that forms the letter "O" within the grid boundaries of 20W and 16H.
