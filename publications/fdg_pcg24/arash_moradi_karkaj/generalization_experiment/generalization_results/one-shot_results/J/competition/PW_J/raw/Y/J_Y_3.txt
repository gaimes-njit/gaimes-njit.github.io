To generate the uppercase letter "Y", we can use the following stream of `ab_drop()` commands:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
```

Explanation:
1. Place a horizontal straight block (`b31`) at position 1.
2. Place a vertical straight block (`b13`) at position 2.
3. Place another horizontal straight block (`b31`) at position 2.
4. Place a 1x1 square block (`b11`) at position 3.
5. Place another 1x1 square block (`b11`) at position 2.
6. Place another 1x1 square block (`b11`) at position 1.

This sequence of commands will create the uppercase letter "Y" in the Tetris-like environment.
