To generate the uppercase English letter "A", you can follow the stream of ab_drop() commands below:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b13', 2)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
```

These commands will place the blocks in a Tetris-like environment according to the gravity rules, creating the letter "A".
