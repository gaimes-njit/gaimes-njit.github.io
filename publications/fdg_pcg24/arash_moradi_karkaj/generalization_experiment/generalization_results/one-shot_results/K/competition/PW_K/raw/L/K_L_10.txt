To generate the uppercase letter "L", you can use the following stream of commands:

```
ab_drop('b11',1)    # Place a 1x1 block at position 1
ab_drop('b11',1)    # Place another 1x1 block at position 1
ab_drop('b11',1)    # Place another 1x1 block at position 1
ab_drop('b11',1)    # Place another 1x1 block at position 1
ab_drop('b13',1)    # Place a vertical straight block at position 1
ab_drop('b31',2)    # Place a horizontal straight block at position 2
ab_drop('b31',3)    # Place another horizontal straight block at position 3
```

This will form the uppercase letter "L" in the grid.
