To generate the letter X, the following sequence of ab_drop() commands can be used:

ab_drop('b11', 1)  # Place a square block at position 1
ab_drop('b11', 6)  # Place a square block at position 6
ab_drop('b11', 2)  # Place a square block at position 2
ab_drop('b11', 5)  # Place a square block at position 5
ab_drop('b31', 3)  # Place a horizontal straight block at position 3
ab_drop('b13', 4)  # Place a vertical straight block at position 4
ab_drop('b11', 2)  # Place a square block at position 2
ab_drop('b11', 5)  # Place a square block at position 5
ab_drop('b11', 1)  # Place a square block at position 1
ab_drop('b11', 6)  # Place a square block at position 6

This sequence of commands will create the letter X in the Tetris-like environment with the given rules.
