To generate the uppercase English letter "S", we can use the following ab_drop() commands:

```plaintext
ab_drop('b31',1)
ab_drop('b31',2)
ab_drop('b31',3)
ab_drop('b13',1)
ab_drop('b31',1)
ab_drop('b31',2)
ab_drop('b31',3)
ab_drop('b13',-1)
ab_drop('b31',3)
ab_drop('b31',4)
ab_drop('b31',5)
ab_drop('b13',2)
```

These commands will stack the appropriate block types in the grid to form the letter "S" shape. Please note that the order and position of the block types are important to achieve the desired shape and stability.
