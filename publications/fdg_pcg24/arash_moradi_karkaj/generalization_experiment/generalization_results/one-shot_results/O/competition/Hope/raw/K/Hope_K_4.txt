To create the structure of the letter "K" inside a 20*16 box, we can use the following ab_drop commands:

ab_drop('b11', 0)
ab_drop('b11', 0)
ab_drop('b11', 0)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b31', 9)
ab_drop('b11', 15)
ab_drop('b11', 15)
ab_drop('b11', 15)

This will create the letter "K" structure, where b11 represents a 1*1 block, b13 represents a 1*3 block, and b31 represents a 3*1 block with the center as the block position. The x positions are given based on the starting point of the box area, which is (0,0).
