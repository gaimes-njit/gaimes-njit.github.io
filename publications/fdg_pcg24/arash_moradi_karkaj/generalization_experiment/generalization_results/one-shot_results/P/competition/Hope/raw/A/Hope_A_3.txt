To create the structure of the letter "A" inside a 20x16 box, you can use the following ab_drop commands:

ab_drop('b13', 7)  # top horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b13', 8)  # top horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b13', 9)  # top horizontal line of A

ab_drop('b31', 6)  # left vertical line of A
ab_drop('b31', 10)  # right vertical line of A

ab_drop('b31', 7)  # center horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b31', 8)  # center horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b31', 9)  # center horizontal line of A

ab_drop('b31', 11)  # bottom horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b31', 12)  # bottom horizontal line of A
ab_drop('b31', 13)  # bottom horizontal line of A

This sequence of commands will drop the blocks in the specified positions to form the letter "A" inside the 20x16 box.
