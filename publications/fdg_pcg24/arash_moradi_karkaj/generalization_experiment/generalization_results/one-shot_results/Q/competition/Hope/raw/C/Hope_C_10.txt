To create the letter "C" inside the 20*16 box, you can use the following sequence of ab_drop commands:

ab_drop('b31', 0)
ab_drop('b13', 0)
ab_drop('b11', 0)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b13', 4)
ab_drop('b31', 4)

This sequence will drop the different blocks from top to bottom, starting from the given x positions, to form the shape of the letter "C" inside the box.
