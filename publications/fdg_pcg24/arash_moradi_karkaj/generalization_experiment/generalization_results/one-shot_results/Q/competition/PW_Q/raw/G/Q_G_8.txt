To create the uppercase letter "G" in the Tetris-like environment, we can use the following stream of ab_drop() commands:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 7)
ab_drop('b31', 4)
ab_drop('b13', 7)
```

These commands will place the block types in the correct positions to form the "G" shape within the allowed space boundaries.
