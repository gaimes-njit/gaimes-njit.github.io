To generate the letter "R" using the mentioned block types, the following ab_drop() commands can be used:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 2)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 1)
```

These commands will sequentially drop the blocks in the grid, resulting in the formation of the letter "R".
