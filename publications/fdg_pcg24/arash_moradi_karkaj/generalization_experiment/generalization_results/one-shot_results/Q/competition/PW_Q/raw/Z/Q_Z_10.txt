To generate the uppercase letter "Z", we can use the following ab_drop() commands:

```
ab_drop('b31', 1)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b31', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 7)
ab_drop('b31', 7)
ab_drop('b11', 10)
ab_drop('b31', 10)
ab_drop('b11', 13)
ab_drop('b31', 13)
ab_drop('b11', 16)
ab_drop('b31', 16)
ab_drop('b11', 19)
```

This sequence of commands will place the blocks in a way that resembles the uppercase letter "Z". The blocks are stacked on top of each other using the 2D gravity rules, starting from the left side of the grid.
