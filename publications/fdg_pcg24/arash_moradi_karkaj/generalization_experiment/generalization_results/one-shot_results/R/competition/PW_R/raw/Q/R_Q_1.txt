```
ab_drop("b31", 1)
ab_drop("b31", 4)
ab_drop("b13", 4)
ab_drop("b13", 1)
ab_drop("b11", 2)
ab_drop("b11", 3)
ab_drop("b11", 5)
ab_drop("b11", 6)
ab_drop("b11", 7)
ab_drop("b11", 8)
ab_drop("b11", 9)
ab_drop("b11", 10)
ab_drop("b13", 10)
ab_drop("b13", 7)
ab_drop("b31", 7)
```

These commands will create a stable arrangement of blocks in the shape of the letter "Q" within the allowed grid space.
