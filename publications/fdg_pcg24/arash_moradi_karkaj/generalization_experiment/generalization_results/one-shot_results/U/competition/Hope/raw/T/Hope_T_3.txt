To create a structure exactly like "T" inside a 20*16 box, we can use the following commands:

1. Drop a b31 block at position (8, 6): ab_drop('b31', 8)

Explanation:
- 'b31' represents a block with a width of 3 and a height of 1, centered at the block's position.
- We drop this block at position (8, 6), which will place the center of the block at coordinates (9, 6).
