To generate the uppercase English letter "V" in the defined environment, you can use the following stream of ab_drop() commands:

```
ab_drop('b13',1)
ab_drop('b13',6)
ab_drop('b31',2)
ab_drop('b31',5)
ab_drop('b31',3)
ab_drop('b31',4)
```

This stream of commands will create a stable structure resembling the letter "V" in the grid.
