2. Place a b31 block at position (4, 1) using the ab_drop(31, 4) command.
3. Place a b13 block at position (5, 1) using the ab_drop(3, 5) command.
4. Place a b11 block at position (4, 2) using the ab_drop(1, 4) command.

```
ab_drop(3, 1)
ab_drop(31, 4)
```
