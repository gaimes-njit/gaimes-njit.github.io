Example of the `ab_drop()` function usage to create a stable structure for the capital letter "O":

```python
ab_drop('b13', 9)
ab_drop('b13', 9)
ab_drop('b31', 8)
ab_drop('b31', 10)
ab_drop('b31', 7)
ab_drop('b31', 11)
ab_drop('b13', 9)
ab_drop('b13', 9)
```

This sequence of `ab_drop()` function calls drops blocks of the specified type (`'b11'`, `'b31'`, or `'b13'`) at the given `x_position` to form a stable structure resembling the letter "O".
