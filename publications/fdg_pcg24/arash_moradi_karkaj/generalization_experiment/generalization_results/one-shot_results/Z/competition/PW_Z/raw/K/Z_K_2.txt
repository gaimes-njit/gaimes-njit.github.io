To generate the uppercase English letter "K" using the mentioned command and Block Types, you can follow the sequence of ab_drop() commands below:

ab_drop('b31', 1)  # Place the first horizontal block at position 1
ab_drop('b11', 1)  # Place the corner square block at position 1
ab_drop('b13', 2)  # Place the vertical block at position 2
ab_drop('b31', 2)  # Place the second horizontal block at position 2
ab_drop('b31', 3)  # Place the third horizontal block at position 3
ab_drop('b11', 3)  # Place the corner square block at position 3

This sequence of commands will create the uppercase English letter "K" within the defined environment and gravity rules.
