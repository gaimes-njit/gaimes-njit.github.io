The following stream of ab_drop() commands can be used to generate the uppercase English letter "A":

```
ab_drop('b13', 1)
ab_drop('b13', 4)
ab_drop('b13', 7)
ab_drop('b13', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 3)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 6)
ab_drop('b31', 2)
ab_drop('b31', 5)
ab_drop('b31', 8)
ab_drop('b31', 3)
ab_drop('b31', 4)
ab_drop('b31', 5)
ab_drop('b31', 6)
ab_drop('b13', 2)
ab_drop('b13', 8)
ab_drop('b13', 3)
ab_drop('b13', 7)
ab_drop('b13', 4)
ab_drop('b13', 6)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
ab_drop('b11', 4)
ab_drop('b11', 5)
```

The above commands will place the blocks in a way that forms the shape of the letter "A" within the boundaries of the allowed space.
