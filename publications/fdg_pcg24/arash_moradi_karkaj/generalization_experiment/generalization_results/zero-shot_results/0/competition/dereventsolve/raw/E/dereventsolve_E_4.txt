To generate the structure that looks like the letter "E", we can use the following sequence of `ab_drop` function calls:

```
ab_drop("top_left")
ab_drop("middle_left")
ab_drop("bottom_left")
ab_drop("top")
ab_drop("middle")
ab_drop("bottom")
ab_drop("top_right")
ab_drop("middle_right")
ab_drop("bottom_right")
```

This sequence will create a structure similar to the letter "E".
